Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Reppo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a workout tracking app for iOS. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data Storage

All of your workout data, exercise history, bodyweight entries, templates, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that store your personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or Apple Health data.
  • We do not create user accounts or require sign-in.

In-App Purchases

Reppo uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and one-time purchases. RevenueCat processes transactions through Apple's App Store infrastructure. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt data from Apple — it does not receive your name, email, or other personal details from us. You can review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more information.

Backup & Export

You can export your workout data as a .reppobackup file or import data from CSV. These files are created and stored locally on your device. Sharing or transferring these files is entirely under your control — we never access them.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (anonymous purchase data only).
  • Apple App Store — Handles payment processing for in-app purchases.

We do not integrate any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Reppo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, contact us at feedback@reppo.app.
